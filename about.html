About

Hi, I’m Sean.

I’m a customer-focused engineering leader who helps build high-performing engineering teams that work with greater purpose and less toil. Better outcomes for customers, the business, and your staff.

I provide fractional and interim engineering technology and management leadership services. I can help when teams need more engineering depth, an additional set of eyes, or more bandwidth.

I help engineers put customers front and center to build high-performing engineering teams that work with greater purpose and less toil.
